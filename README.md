A guide and collection of resources on how to mod and flash BIOS to HP Z440, Z640, and Z840. The flashing procedure is done with the SOIC8 clip, and no BIOS chip clips or desoldering. The modded BIOS files ReSizable Bar, and turbo unlock on v3 Xeons.


**Disclaimer: I am not responsible if you do brick your computer - you are doing this at your own risk. Keep plenty of backups.**
